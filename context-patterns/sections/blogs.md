# Blogs

Blogs provide timely, practical insights into context engineering from practitioners building production systems. Unlike academic papers and formal courses, blogs capture the latest techniques, war stories, and evolving best practices from the front lines of AI engineering.

This section curates the most influential blogs and individual posts on context engineering, organized by publication and topic.

## Key Blog Publications

| Publication | Focus | Frequency | 
|---|---|---|
| **Anthropic Blog** | Official Claude updates, research, and guides | Weekly |
| **OpenAI Blog** | Platform updates and capabilities | Weekly |

| **LangChain Blog** | LLM application patterns | Weekly |
